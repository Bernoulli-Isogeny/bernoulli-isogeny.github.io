Where ? When ?

The workshop will be held at the Bernoulli center, at EPFL in Lausanne during the week of the 16-20 November 2026.

Aim of the workshop

  1. Initiate cryptographers to isogenies of abelian varieties, and reversely, initiating expert on isogenies of abelian varieties to cryptographic considerations.

  2. Share the current state-of-the-art results on isogenies of abelian varieties and their applications to isogeny based cryptography.

  3. Discuss the open research directions.

  4. Initiate a working group towards the native support in Python/Sagemath of easy to use dimension 2 and 4 isogenies.
